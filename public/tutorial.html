NotChess

Have you ever played normal chess?
Well, this is the exact opposite.
Your goal is to lose all pieces
to your opponent.


The rules are the following:

  • Capturing enemy pieces, when
    possible, is compulsory
  • When more than one capture is
    available, you can choose which one
  • The king is just a pawn on steroids,
    so it doesn't have any royal power!
  • There is no check or checkmate
  • There is no castling
  • There is no pawn promotion
  • You win when you lose all your pieces
    or when you are stalemated (no
    valid moves left).

We know this might feel strange for
you, but don't worry, that's exactly
the purpose of this chess variant -
make you question all your chess
knowledge
We wish you BAD LUCK!
