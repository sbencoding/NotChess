NotChess

NotChess is an initiative in the
chess community that aims to
bring a more beginner-friendly
approach to the sometimes too
serious chess environment.


As a new initiative, resources are
hard to come by. And to bring
this mission to the community
we will need the support of as
many people as we can!
Of course, only donate if it is
financially feasable for you to do so!

Any ammount you wish to donate
is greatly appreciated, and we will
try to make the best use of it in
bringing this mission forward.
Thank you for you generosity.
Best of luck in your matches!
- The NotChess Team
